# Business Insights

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer behavior, sales performance, and operational metrics for an Indian e-commerce business. The analysis was performed using Python for data preprocessing, MySQL for SQL analysis, and Power BI for interactive dashboard development.

---

# Dashboard Insights

## 1. Sales Overview

### Key Findings
- Monthly revenue trends highlight seasonal sales patterns.
- Certain product categories generate significantly higher revenue than others.
- Marketing channels contribute differently to revenue generation.
- Revenue KPIs provide a quick overview of business performance.

### Business Impact
- Helps management identify high-performing categories.
- Supports marketing budget allocation.
- Tracks overall business growth.

---

## 2. Sales & Operations

### Key Findings
- Revenue varies across different locations.
- Payment methods contribute differently to total revenue.
- Device usage influences order volume.
- Discount patterns affect sales performance.

### Business Impact
- Optimize payment options.
- Improve regional sales strategies.
- Focus operational efforts on high-performing markets.

---

## 3. Customer Behavior & Engagement

### Key Findings
- Funnel analysis identifies customer drop-off during the purchase journey.
- Purchase Rate measures conversion efficiency.
- Cart Abandonment Rate highlights lost sales opportunities.
- Session duration and browsing behavior indicate customer engagement.

### Business Impact
- Improve website usability.
- Reduce cart abandonment.
- Increase customer conversion.

---

## 4. Product Performance & Customer Feedback

### Key Findings
- Product categories receive different customer ratings.
- Customer reviews provide satisfaction indicators.
- Helpful votes identify valuable customer feedback.
- Marketing channels influence customer satisfaction.

### Business Impact
- Improve product quality.
- Prioritize highly rated product categories.
- Use customer feedback for continuous improvement.

---

# Overall Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing marketing channels.
- Improve customer journey to reduce cart abandonment.
- Focus promotions on top-performing product categories.
- Strengthen regional marketing strategies.
- Improve customer satisfaction through review analysis.
- Optimize payment methods based on customer preference.

---

# Technical Implementation

## Data Processing
- Cleaned and transformed raw e-commerce data using Python (Pandas).
- Performed feature engineering and data preprocessing.

## Database Analysis
- Imported the cleaned dataset into MySQL.
- Wrote SQL queries to analyze sales, customer behavior, products, and operational performance.
- Executed SQL scripts using Visual Studio Code with the MySQL extension.

## Dashboard Development
- Imported the processed dataset into Power BI.
- Created DAX measures for KPIs.
- Built four interactive dashboard pages.
- Added slicers for dynamic filtering.
- Applied consistent formatting and visualization best practices.
