# Business Problem

## Project Title
Indian E-Commerce Customer Behavior Analysis

## Business Scenario

An Indian e-commerce company has experienced slower revenue growth despite increasing website traffic. Management wants to understand customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and operational performance to improve business decisions.

As a Data Analyst, your responsibility is to analyze customer, product, order, and payment data to identify trends, measure key performance indicators (KPIs), and provide actionable business recommendations.

## Objectives

- Increase revenue
- Improve customer retention
- Reduce cart abandonment
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Identify top-performing products
- Optimize payment methods
- Understand regional performance

## Stakeholders

- CEO
- Sales Team
- Marketing Team
- Operations Team
- Product Team

## Success Metrics

- Revenue Growth
- Conversion Rate
- Average Order Value
- Customer Retention Rate
- Cart Abandonment Rate
- Customer Satisfaction Score
